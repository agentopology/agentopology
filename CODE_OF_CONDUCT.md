# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Trolling, insulting comments, personal or political attacks
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
